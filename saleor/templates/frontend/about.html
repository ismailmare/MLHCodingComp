ABOUT US

StudentsEat is an on-campus food delivery service which delivers local food and beverages directly to any students’ location within a campus. StudentsEat was founded by a group of students in late 2017, who recognized a better way for students to balance their hunger and studies.

Direct Delivery

With us, you will never need to leave your seat for food again. Simply let us know where you’re studying, and we’ll deliver your food directly to you. Save time by not having to pack your bags and trek across your campus.

Food. Fast.

Nobody wants to wait an hour for their food to arrive. With knowledgeable Student Agents delivering your food, you can trust in their ability to grab and deliver your order – fast.

Less Fees

Living the student life and paying tuition fees is not easy. Let’s not complicate life even more by forcing you to pay five-dollar delivery fees. StudentsEat is committed to keeping delivery fees as low as possible so that you do not feel guilty about taking care of your hunger at school.
